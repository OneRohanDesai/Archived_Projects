Tomato Garlic Rice
• 1 cup rice        • 3 tomatoes
• 4 garlic cloves   • 1 onion
• Chili flakes      • Olive oil

1. Fry garlic + onion in oil until golden
2. Add chopped tomatoes, cook 5 min
3. Mix in rice + 2 cups water + chili
4. Simmer 12 min. Done.

Full local observability stack (Prometheus + Grafana + Loki + Vault) runs in background. Watch your SLOs while you eat.
